# Campus to Market — Why We Are Ideal to Solve This

---

I built this app because I am the customer.

I have practiced mindfulness for close to a decade, with over 1,200 hours logged — and that is only what one app recorded. Long before I started building, I was already the person cobbling together a timer, a music player, and mental notes just to run a session that felt like mine. I knew exactly what was missing, because I had been missing it for years.

That practitioner's perspective shapes every decision in the product. I did not design for a beginner who needs hand-holding. I designed for someone who knows what they want and just needs a tool that gets out of the way.

On the technical side, I built the entire app solo — a production-quality iOS application in SwiftUI, with a custom audio engine, block-by-block session tracking, ambient sound mixing, and a full test suite. I designed and documented a cloud marketplace backend (FastAPI, AWS, Cognito, Terraform) for the next phase of the product. I have the skills to build what I am describing, and I have already built most of it.

Most founders in the wellness space understand either the practice or the engineering. I understand both from the inside. That is a hard combination to replicate, and it is why this product exists at the depth it does.
