# Campus to Market — Market Potential

---

## Top-Down

Global meditation app market: **$2.2B in 2025**, growing at **15% CAGR** to **$7B by 2033** (Grand View Research). North America: **~$900M today**, projected **$6.1B by 2035** (MarkWide Research).

Target segment — experienced practitioners who have outgrown guided-content apps — is roughly **15–20% of active users**: approximately **$135–180M SAM** in North America.

---

## Bottom-Up

**Canada:**
- 552,000 identified meditation app users (Start.io, Feb 2026)
- 20% are experienced practitioners = **110,000 people**
- 10% adopt at $4.99/month = **11,000 users → ~$660K/year**

**US (~10× Canada):**
- 37.9M meditators (NIH, 2022); experienced practitioners ~1.5–2M
- 10% adoption = **~$9–12M annual potential**

---

## TAM / SAM / SOM

**TAM** — $2.2B (2025) → $7B (2033), global meditation apps.

**SAM** — ~$135–180M, experienced practitioners in North America.

**SOM** — ~$1–2M, Canada + US, years 1–3.

US adults practicing meditation have **tripled since 2012** (NIH). The market is still expanding.
