# Campus to Market — Top Three Goals for Validate

---

**1. Test my core assumption with real customers.**

I have a prototype and a problem hypothesis, but I have not interviewed practitioners outside my own experience. The 50 customer interviews will tell me whether the fragmentation problem is real and widespread, or specific to me — and whether the ritual builder is the right response to it.

**2. Build a value proposition that works with strangers.**

My pitch makes sense to me because I lived the problem. The BMC workshops will force me to articulate it to people with no context and find where it breaks down. I want a one-sentence value proposition that holds up in a cold conversation.

**3. Decide with evidence whether this is a business.**

Meditation Builder could be a paid iOS app, a subscription tool, or a marketplace for practitioners to share routines. These require different strategies. Right now I am building without choosing. The coaching and validation process will tell me which path has real demand — or whether there is no business case at all.
