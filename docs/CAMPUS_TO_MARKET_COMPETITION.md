# Campus to Market — Competition and Alternatives

---

## What customers do today

Experienced practitioners combine 2–3 tools:
- **A content app** (Calm, Headspace, Insight Timer) for guided audio or ambient sound
- **A timer app** (Timefully, MindTime) for interval bells and structure
- **A notes app** for logging

Switching between these adds 3–8 minutes of setup and breaks focus before the session starts.

---

## Alternatives and where they fall short

**Calm / Headspace** — Built for beginners. Subscription content libraries. No custom session sequencing.

**Insight Timer** — 200,000+ free tracks. Best retention in category (16% D30) via community features. Still content-first; no block-based builder.

**Timefully / MindTime** — Closest to a builder. Support interval bells and presets. No ambient sound mixing, no per-block session history, no routine library.

**Oak / Waking Up** — Single-author content. No user composition.

---

## The gap

No app lets a practitioner sequence multiple block types — silence, chanting, breathwork — with custom bells between each, save the routine, and review per-block timing afterward. Customers either accept a generic session or build a manual workaround.

Meditation Builder is that workaround, productized.
